Protect Your Exterior from Water Damage

Choose us for roof soft washing and gutter cleaning in and around Wake and Franklin and surrounding areas, NC.

You might not realize that your roof requires cleaning, but regular maintenance is essential to prolong its lifespan and remove any algae or mold buildup. Turn to Falcon Pressure Wash for roof soft washing services in the Wake and Franklin, NC area. We utilize a gentle chemical solution combined with low water pressure to clean your roof safely.

We’ll begin with an on-site assessment and provide a free estimate before washing your roof. Once the job is complete, we’ll either show you our work in person or send you before-and-after photos if you’re unavailable.

Contact us today to schedule your roof soft washing services!

If water accumulates, it can overflow and create puddles around your property’s foundation, jeopardizing its stability. We’ll clear your gutters to prevent this issue.

Our gutter cleaning process includes:

  • Removing twigs, leaves, and other debris by hand or with a vacuum
  • Flushing the gutters and downspouts with water to ensure proper drainage and eliminate any remaining debris
  • Cleaning up after the job to leave your property in better condition than we found it
